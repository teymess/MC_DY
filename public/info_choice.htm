You now have the opportunity to receive information about air pollution levels and the number of life years lost because of air pollution in a specific region.


On the next screen, you will be asked to indicate which of two regions you prefer to read about.



How will your choice be implemented?

The computer program will draw a ball from a virtual urn containing 6 green balls and 4 red balls.
- If the computer draws a green ball, you will receive information about your preferred region.
- If the computer draws a red ball, you will receive information about the other region.

In summary, you are more likely to receive information about your preferred region.
