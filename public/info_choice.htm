On the next screen, you will be asked to indicate whether you prefer to receive information about air pollution levels and the number of life years lost because of air pollution in your home county, or whether you prefer not to receive any information.


How will your choice be implemented?

Whether your preferred choice is implemented or not is determined according to the following procedure:

The computer program will draw a ball from a virtual urn containing 6 green balls and 4 red balls.
  • If the computer draws a green ball, your preferred choice will be implemented. (If you chose to receive the information, you will receive it. If you chose not to receive the information, you will not receive it.)
  • If the computer draws a red ball, your preferred choice will not be implemented. (If you chose to receive the information, you will not receive it. If you chose not to receive it, you will receive it.)

In summary, your preferred choice is more likely to be implemented.
