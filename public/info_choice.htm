On the next screen, you will be asked whether you want to receive information about air pollution levels and the number of life years lost because of air pollution in your home district.


In particular, you can choose between:
  • receiving the information
  • not receiving the information

How will your choice be implemented?

The computer program will draw a ball from a virtual urn containing 6 green balls and 4 red balls.
  • If the computer draws a green ball, your choice will be implemented. (If you chose to receive the information, you will receive it. If you chose to not receive the information, you will not receive it.)
  • If the computer draws a red ball, your choice will not be implemented. (If you chose to receive the information, you will not receive it. If you chose to not receive it, you will receive it.)

In summary, it is more likely that your choice is implemented.
